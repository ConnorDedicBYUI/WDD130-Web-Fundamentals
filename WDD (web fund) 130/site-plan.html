Purpose

My plan is to make a personal website. I will include projects I have done, my resume and qualifications. I will also include a small section for my own personal hobbies and some fun things I have created. Primarily though I would include cybersecurity related things, as that is my career passion and what I want to do. I hope to add guides for cybers things as well. It will serve as a portfolio and show how I am (at least attempting) to progress the field.

Audience

General audience. Mostly for people in the cybersecurity field but will include small parts for friends.
